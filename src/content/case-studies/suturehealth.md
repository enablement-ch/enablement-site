## Initial Situation & Challenge

Michael was the founder, CEO, salesperson, and product manager. SutureHealth had stagnated at $3M ARR for 18 months on inbound alone. *"We really don't have any lead gen systems set up."* The plan was waiting for someone else to build the engine.

## Solution

Outbound engine wired into HubSpot — ICP-scored lead lists, three persona-tuned sequences, reply qualifier routing positive intent directly to Michael's inbox. The system operates independently of his daily involvement.

## Result

12 qualified demos per month within 90 days. ARR run rate moved from $3M to $4.5M in 6 months. Michael moved from doing the outbound to reviewing the outbound. Build + Own engagement — SutureHealth operates the system internally now.
